Welcome to your scheduler

Scheduler is a tool that allows you to keep track of all your events using a clean and simple interface. In the case where there you have many overlapping events you want to attend, Scheduler can help you sort out your options. In a time like this where many classes are moving online, online learners can often feel isolated, so we also created a blog post feature that allows people to share their online learning experience and connect with other online learners.
